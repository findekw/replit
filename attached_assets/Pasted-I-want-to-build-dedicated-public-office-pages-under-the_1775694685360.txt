Each real estate office should have its own public page inside the platform, such as:
- /alfintas
- /dar-alkhaleej
- /prime-home

This page must feel like a mini landing page for the office, but still under the main platform brand.

Requirements:

1) Add office slug system
- Each office must have a unique slug

  - Office name: مكتب الفنطاس العقاري
